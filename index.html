What is this?

The Restorative Well is a collection of anonymous stories of gratitude from around the world.

How should I use it?

Visitors of the restorative well can read the different messages of gratitude to help bring a positive and calming effect to their current situation. Find a piece of gratitude that makes you smile. Consider leaving a note of gratitude for the next visitor.

The Restorative Well is for visitors of all ages, cultures, and identities.
